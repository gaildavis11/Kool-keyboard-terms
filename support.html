How to enable Kool Keyboard

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Go to General → Keyboard → Keyboards
  3. Tap Add New Keyboard…
  4. Select Kool Keyboard
  5. Tap Kool Keyboard again to manage settings
  6. Open any app and tap the globe key to switch keyboards

About “Allow Full Access”

Kool Keyboard does not collect what you type. If you choose to enable Allow Full Access, it is only used to support optional features (such as improved customization/settings availability). Typed content is never collected or transmitted.
